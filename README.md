# Capstone_1
First Capstone for DATA. Focusing on Data Storytelling, SQL, and Excel

I am a data analyst for a growing business called EmporiUm. It is a student bookstore chain that sells tech products, art supplies, and other products both in-store and online. My sales manager, Erbayne Middleton, asked me to find the overall sales for our territory, the month-to-month revenue breakdown, compare total revenue for the sales territory and its region, number of transactions per month, and average transaction size by product category, provide ranking for in-store performance for each store, and lastly, where to focus sales attention for the next quarter.
